ASI’s CheeTah is a hybrid pixel detector for electron microscopy applications. Enabling the full power of Medipix 3 and Timepix 3 technology, the CheeTah detector allows single electron sensitivity. Moreover, high-speed and noiseless data acquisition. Also, it offers unprecedented possibilities to boost the power of standard TEMs.
The EM – CheeTah is the detector of choice for low-dose applications and for beam-sensitive materials!


The CheeTah can be mounted on any kind of TEM like ThermoFisher, JEOL, Hitachi and Zeiss.

Key Features

Direct Electron Detection
The HybridPixelDetector smallest pixel size, 55 µm
Radiation hard (Giga Electronvolt) and No Maintenance
Zero-Noise and Single Electron Detection
Virtual BF, DF, ADF, HAADF detectors
Superior High Speed
