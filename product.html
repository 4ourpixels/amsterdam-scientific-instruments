Timepix3-based detection systems

Here's why our event-based and data driven detectors stand out

Unmatched Speed
Experience unparalleled acquisition speed with frame rates ranging from 200MHz to 600MHz. Our pixel readout, developed in collaboration with NIKHEF, operates independently for each pixel, eliminating deadtime and ensuring ultra-fast and precise detection.
Dynamic Range
Capture a staggering 300 million hits per second, enabling you to detect photons, neutrons, ions, gamma particles, and even alpha particles.
Flexible Configuration
Choose from a variety of configurations, including front-facing and L-shaped setups, to meet your specific experimental needs.
Seamless Data Integration
Enjoy unmatched data flexibility with our detectors. Our flexible data formats and comprehensive API facilitate effortless integration into your existing workflows.
