Quantam Science

Key Features

Discover the advantages of Timepix3's pixel-by-pixel tracking and time-stamped data, offering unparalleled insights compared to conventional frame-based alternatives.

Light sensitive sensor, with a conversion efficiency > 99%
Wavelength rage: 400 - 1000 nm
55 μm pixel size. Achieve a sub-pixel spatial resolution with centroiding (< 10 μm)
Simultaneous detection of arrival time and intensity in each individual pixel
Choose from a variety of configurations and designs, including front-facing and L-shaped setups, to meet your specific experimental needs.
Event-based detection: Real-time and continuous operation with up to 80 million events per second
Time resolution of 1.56 ns which can reach the picosecond range using TDC signals
Flexible integration via Python API and a GUI for quick visualization and detector control

None of these options suit your experiment? Design your own!

The Chronos solution team can configure the detector to match the application-specific needs. Together we can create the ideal detection system for your experiment.
